What fraction is shaded?

Area of quarter circles $/$ area of outer circle: $$ {2 \cdot \frac14 \pi h^2 \over \pi ({\frac12 c})^2} = {2 h^2 \over c^2} $$ The following holds for
any right-angled triangle: $$ {c^2 \over h^2} = 2 + {p \over q} + {q \over p} $$ In this case ${p \over q} = \frac12$, so $$ {c^2 \over h^2} = 2 + \frac12 + \frac21 = \frac92 $$ and thus $$ {2 h^2 \over c^2} = {\bf \frac49} $$
